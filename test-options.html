How to Access the Options Page:

After loading the extension in Chrome:

  1. Click on the extension icon in Chrome toolbar
  2. Right-click and select "Options" OR
  3. Go to chrome://extensions
  4. Find "Nxtscape Agent" and click "Details"
  5. Click "Extension options"
